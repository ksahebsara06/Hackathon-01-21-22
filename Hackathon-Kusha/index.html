The Definition

In Physical Sciences, Mechanical Energy is the SUM of Potential and Kinetic energy within a closed system.

Misconceptions

In exams, this concept is often tested on because the Law of Conservation of Energy is used frequently in calculations found in energy calculations. Common errors include taking the difference between the potential and kinetic energy in the system instead of the summation.
